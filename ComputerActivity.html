(CAUSES)

*Technological Advancements

*Social & Communication Benefits

*Security & Safety

Understanding these causes is important because they show how AI improves technology, makes life easier, boosts productivity, drives economic growth, enhances communication, and strengthens security, shaping a more efficient and connected world.
(Positive Effects)

*Increased Efficiency & Productivity

*Enhanced Convenience

*Improved Healthcare

*Better Communication & Accessibility

*Increaed Safety & Security
Understanding the causes of AI helps us see why it was created, how it enhances efficiency, convenience, and security, and what potential challenges it may bring, enabling us to adapt, innovate, and use AI responsibly for a better future.
